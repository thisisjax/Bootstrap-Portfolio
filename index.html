About Me

My name is Jacqueline Demers, I was born and raised in Orlando, FL. I started my journey at Stetson University in graphic arts. A year later i decided to change my career path and I switched over to Hospitality Management! My goal was to eventually get into Event Planning aftr getting my AA in Hospitality Management however, those plans changed as well. For the past 5 years I have started several small businesses. With these business I have had to use my graphic design background to make logos, flyers, t-shirts & so many other things that come along with advertising and marketing your business. I have always been interested in getting back to my roots in digital arts and this web course is going to be exciting & new for me!
